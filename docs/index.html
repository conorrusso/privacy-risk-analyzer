WHAT IS THIS

Most TPRM programs evaluate vendors on security controls — SOC 2 reports, pen tests, vulnerability management. Almost none of them evaluate privacy risk — what vendors actually do with your data, and what protections exist for your data subjects.

privacy-risk-analyzer is an open source n8n workflow that applies The Privacy Lens Framework to vendor artifacts — scoring them across 8 dimensions, routing findings to legal with AI-drafted contract language, and logging evidence automatically.

Built for GRC teams without dedicated privacy counsel. Especially useful for teams rapidly onboarding AI vendors.

02
THE PRIVACY LENS FRAMEWORK

◈ HOVER EACH DIMENSION TO SEE WHAT CLAUDE EVALUATES ◈

🗜️ DATA MINIMIZATION GDPR Art. 5(1)(c)

Does the vendor collect only what is necessary? Are retention windows defined?

🚩 Vague "business purposes" · no retention window

🔗 SUB-PROCESSOR TRANSPARENCY GDPR Art. 28(2)

Is a sub-processor list published? Are customers notified of changes?

🚩 No list · no change notification · broad "third parties"

🧑‍⚖️ DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS GDPR Art. 15–22 · CCPA

Can users exercise DSAR, deletion, portability, and correction rights?

🚩 No DSAR process · no deletion mechanism · no timelines

🌍 TRANSFER MECHANISMS GDPR Chapter V

Are cross-border transfers covered by SCCs, BCRs, or adequacy decisions?

🚩 No transfer basis · reliance on invalidated Privacy Shield

🚨 BREACH NOTIFICATION GDPR Art. 33

What are notification timelines and obligations to customers?

🚩 "Reasonable time" language · no customer notification obligation

🤖 AI/ML DATA USAGE EU AI Act · GDPR Art. 22

Is customer data used to train models? Is there an opt-out? Who are AI sub-processors?

🚩 No training opt-out · "improve services" language · AI sub-processors undisclosed

🗑️ RETENTION & DELETION GDPR Art. 5(1)(e) · Art. 17

Are deletion SLAs defined? What happens to data on contract termination?

🚩 No deletion SLA · no secure deletion standard · retained after termination

📄 DPA COMPLETENESS GDPR Art. 28

Does the DPA include all required Art. 28 elements: subject matter, duration, nature, obligations?

🚩 Missing processor obligations · no audit rights · no sub-processor controls

03
RISK ROUTING
🔴 HIGH RISK
Any dimension scores 1 · OR weighted avg < 2.5
  • Jira ticket created → Priority: HIGH
  • Legal email with Contract Negotiation Brief
  • Slack alert → #legal-privacy-review
  • Claude drafts suggested contract language
  • GRC vendor record → HIGH tier
🟠 MEDIUM RISK
Weighted avg 2.5–3.5 · no dimension below 2
  • Targeted follow-up questionnaire generated
  • Email sent to vendor (gaps only)
  • Jira ticket → Priority: MEDIUM
  • GRC vendor record → MEDIUM tier
🟢 LOW RISK
Weighted avg > 3.5 · no dimension below 3
  • AI summary logged as GRC evidence
  • Vendor record → LOW tier
  • Requestor notified with clean summary
  • Fully automated — zero manual triage
🤖 AI VENDOR ESCALATION
AI/ML vendor detected — regardless of score
  • Auto-escalate to GRC + Legal simultaneously
  • AI-specific DPA addendum questions generated
  • Slack alert → #ai-governance
  • Jira ticket tagged: AI-Vendor
